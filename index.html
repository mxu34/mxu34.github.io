Mengdi Xu

I am a final-year Ph. D. student advised by Ding Zhao (SafeAI Lab) at Carnegie Mellon University. I have spent wonderful summers at Google DeepMind, MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab and Toyota Research Institute. My reseach focuses on generalizable robot learning -- building robots that generalize to new environments with strong adaptivity and robustness.

I received my Master degree in Machine Learning from Machine Learning Department at CMU, and my Master degree in Robotics from LCSR at Johns Hopkins University, supervised by Prof. Gregory S. Chirikjian. I did my undergraduate study at Tsinghua University.
