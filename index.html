Research Interest

I am broadly interested in generalizable robot learning, especially building robots that generalize to challenging unseen environments with strong adaptability and robustness. I currently research from three perspectives:

  • unsupervised reinforcement learning (RL) discovering task structures in transitions, geometries, and policies,
  • distributionally robust RL that balance performance and robustness when facing task uncertainties,
  • few-shot robot learning with largely pretrained foundation models.
